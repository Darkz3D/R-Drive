RAT Token Buyback Strategy

As part of our commitment to creating a sustainable and rewarding ecosystem for RAT holders, we have implemented a buyback strategy that directly supports our community and boosts long-term token value.

Using Pump.fun as a price monitoring platform, the dev team will actively track market dips. During periods of significant price movement or undervaluation, we will allocate buyback funds to purchase tokens from the market.

These buybacks will help to stabilize the token price and reward our loyal RAT holders. The repurchased tokens may be used for burns, staking rewards, or future development initiatives, ensuring that every buyback strengthens the ecosystem.

This strategy is designed to reduce selling pressure, increase scarcity, and reinforce community confidence. Together, we will build a sustainable, decentralized financial future.

Join us in the rebellion. Every buyback is a step closer to empowering the 99%.
