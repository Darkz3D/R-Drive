RAT Staking Platform Strategy

The RAT staking platform is designed to incentivize long-term loyalty and reward holders who believe in the project's vision. By leveraging market dynamics, the platform aims to turn moments of short-term undervaluation into opportunities for growth and community empowerment.

When jeeters dump and the market experiences price dips, the dev team will strategically use these moments to repurchase tokens from the open market. These buybacks not only support price stability but also provide a mechanism to reward loyal stakers.

Repurchased tokens will be redirected toward the staking platform to strengthen the ecosystem. This approach ensures that short-term sell-offs contribute to the long-term development of the project, aligning with our mission of building a sustainable, decentralized future.

The staking rewards will directly reflect community loyalty, encouraging holders to stay committed while allowing the project to capitalize on market fluctuations for further development.
