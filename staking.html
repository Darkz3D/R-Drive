RAT Staking Platform Strategy

To create a sustainable and rewarding ecosystem for RAT holders, the dev team is working on implementing a staking platform that incentivizes long-term commitment and rewards community engagement.

By leveraging price movement data from Pump.fun, we will design a mechanism where dips are identified as opportunities to reward stakers and support token value. Staking will offer benefits like yield rewards, NFT airdrops, and additional influence over future project developments.

The platform will integrate features to allow holders to stake tokens in exchange for rewards generated from buyback opportunities. This ensures that every participant benefits from our commitment to building a decentralized and scalable ecosystem.

Stay tuned for more updates on staking features and reward structures.
